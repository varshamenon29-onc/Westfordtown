# Westford 2050: AI-Powered Community Energy Hub

## Problem
By 2050, towns will need more electricity because of AI, cloud computing, electric vehicles, homes, schools, and businesses.

## Solution
Our project proposes an AI-powered community energy hub that manages renewable energy, battery storage, data center demand, and emergency needs.

## What the Simulator Shows
- Solar power
- Wind power
- Battery level
- Town energy demand
- Data center demand
- AI energy decision
- Cost and carbon savings

## Tech Used
- Python
- Streamlit
- Pandas

## Team Goal
Build a smarter, cleaner, more reliable energy system for Westford that could be copied by other towns.
